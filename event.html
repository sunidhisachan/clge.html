QUANTA

QUANTA portrays the vivid spectrum of Electronics and Communication engineering and how it is affecting our every-day lives. Whether it is the latest and the best in R&D, lectures by some final year students on myriad fields like Robotics,Communication,PLD's,V.L.S.I with innovative and exciting competitions or workshops on riveting topics, they are all to be found in the coming activities of QUANTA club. It is of utmost importance to realize that boundaries are mere perceptions. The shattering of constraints and barriers imposed on oneself is the first step to realizing the boundless infinity of the fascinating world of Electronics & Communication. Creative thought and vision sow the seed of progress - but it is opportunity and constant endeavour which nourishes development.

VERVE

JSS Cultural club is a student body that is responsible for all the cultural events in the Institute around the year. It comprises various sections which take care of the various fields of cultural events. ABESEC cultural club strives to bring out the hidden talent of the students of the college. It is the place where students can showcase what they have got. It helps to discover their own talent. Cultural events include competitions such as instrumental, singing, dance, fashion show etc. The competitive academic environment and co-curricular activities on campus keep the students on their toes. But everyone needs some time off, to relax and recharge. Cultural Clubs at JSS is the best place for this.

ILLUMINATI

The dramatics club is home to all the theatre lovers and nukkad enthusiasts. It gives you the freedom of being whoever you want to be. Discover the craziest person in you, and not only get appreciated but also encouraged . Participate in a bunch of amazing events round the year, from stage and street performances to mimes and comedy acts, leaving behind all the stage fear in you and become an amazing outspoken individual you always dreamt of. A plethora of talented individuals join everyyear to take forward JSS rich legacy in dramatics. Club majorly deals with Stage Plays and Nukkad. From writing play, to direction and to performance, members get to learn the skills required for each of these jobs.
