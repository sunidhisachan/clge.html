JSS Academy of Technical Education

Training and Placement Cell

* OVERVIEW

VISION

Training & Placement Centre endeavours to foster long term relationship with companies across industries. Relationships built over a period of time will result in more students from the Academy getting opportunities for internship and ultimately employment opportunities with these very companies.

MISSION

Student joining the Academy should pass out from the portals of the college with their both hands full, egree in one hand and at least one job offer of their choice in the other. The Training & Placement Centre, JSSATE Noida facilitates the process of placement of students passing out from the Institute, besides collaborating with leading organizations and institutes in setting up of internship and training program of students. The Centre liaises with various industrial establishments, corporate houses etc which conduct campus interviews and select graduate and post-graduate students from all disciplines. The Training & Placement centre provides the facilities to conduct group discussions, tests and interviews besides catering to other logistics. The centre interacts with many industries in the country, of which nearly 500 companies have been attracted by the college ever since its inception and the companies. The industries which approach the institute are of following genres:

    Core Engineering industries
    Software development & IT enabled services
    Manufacturing Industries
    Consultancy Firms
    Finance Companies
    Management Organisations
The placement runs throughout the academic year commencing the last week of July to subsequent June. Pre-Placement Talks are given by the company at the commencement of each campus drive. Job offers, dates of interviews, selection of candidates etc. are announced by the companies through the Training & Placement centre.
